About QuarkVolt

Engineering the Science of Advanced Energy Storage

QuarkVolt is a deep-tech energy company developing next-generation lithium battery systems, EV charging infrastructure, and smart grid solutions. Our work spans from fundamental materials science to utility-scale industrial deployment across six continents.

Founded by electrochemists and power engineers, QuarkVolt brings rigorous scientific methodology to every system we build — from 450Wh/kg cell chemistries to AI-driven grid management platforms serving national utilities.

With ISO 9001, IEC 62133, UL 9540, and UN 38.3 certifications across our product lines, QuarkVolt systems meet the strictest international standards for energy safety and performance.
